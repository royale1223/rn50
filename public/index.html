Bolgaty palace , is one highly recommended property as our venue. Set on a historic island amidst the serene Kochi backwaters, it offers a unique blend of heritage and "magic" that we believe is essential for this milestone gathering.

Why Bolgatty Palace?

  • Unbeatable Value: At an average cost of ₹10,300 per family (₹5,500 for the room + ₹2,800 for dinner + ₹2,000 hall charges) + GST this option is approximately ₹2,000 cheaper than any other venue of comparable caliber, without compromising on the premium ambience we deserve.
  • Fresh Experience: To avoid the "repetition effect," we are steering away from past locations like Kadavu Resort, Calicut (2022).
  • Logistical Ease: Unlike Vythiri Village in Wayanad, which presents significant logistical risks for a group of our size, Bolgatty is easily accessible while remaining secluded.

We truly feel that we need the charm of Bolgatty to recreate the magic of our college days and the last reunion.
